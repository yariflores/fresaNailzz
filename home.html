About Me


Hi! My name is Emily, and I’m a high school student with a passion for nail artistry and design. What began as a creative hobby has blossomed into a skill I take pride in and continuously strive to improve. I specialize in creating custom nail sets—ranging from sleek, minimalist styles to intricate, bold art—always aiming to craft unique looks tailored to each individual.


Despite a busy school schedule, I am deeply committed to honing my craft. I keep up with current trends, explore new techniques, and experiment with different products. I believe that practice, tutorials, and constructive feedback are essential for growth. My attention to detail, creativity, and dedication to delivering high-quality results are what set me apart.

Working on nails has taught me valuable lessons beyond technique—confidence, patience, and strong time management. I've learned how to communicate with clients, understand their preferences, and bring their visions to life. For me, each nail set isn’t just about beauty; it’s about empowering others and making them feel polished and confident.


Looking ahead, I plan to continue developing my skills and eventually turn my passion into a professional business, all while pursuing further education. Whether you’re seeking timeless elegance or a completely custom design, I’m excited to bring your ideas to life—one nail at a time.

Thank you for visiting and supporting my journey!
